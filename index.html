Veronika at Sixty

a South Pacific birthday voyage
From Veronika & Mark
To our dear family & friends

On the 12th of December 2026, Veronika reaches a grand milestone — her 60th birthday — and we intend to celebrate it in equally grand style: aboard Royal Caribbean's Anthem of the Seas, sailing the South Pacific.

The ship departs Sydney on the evening of December 11th and returns on the morning of December 19th, calling at Nouméa, Port Vila, and Mystery Island along the way — with Veronika's birthday itself celebrated at sea, somewhere in the Coral Sea, which feels exactly right.

We would be delighted if you could join us. Beyond a few planned gatherings — a special birthday dinner among them — the days are yours: everyone does their own thing, and we gather as we wish.
